Problem 3: The Bear

Oh no! You've encountered a bear in the forest!

When the user presses the left arrow key, the bear will move 10 pixels to the left. When the user presses the right arrow key, the bear will move 10 pixels to the right. Up arrow moves the bear up, down arrow moves the bear down.

Any connected clients can also move the bear, and can see the bear move on their screen when another user has moved it.
